About Paws & Claws

Discover the Heart of Animal Welfare

Paws and Claws stands as a beacon of hope and love for animals in need. Nestled in the heart of our community, we're more than just a humane society: we're a family committed to creating lasting bonds between people and pets. Our journey began over two decades ago with a simple yet powerful vision: to ensure every animal finds a loving home and every human experiences the joy of pet companionship.

Embracing Change, Expanding Horizons

With the spirit of renewal, we recently joined forces with UpLift and merged spaces with Cheap Thrills, marking a new chapter in our story. This transformation isn't just physical - it's a reinvigoration of our commitment to animals and the people who love them. Our revamped facility is not just a place for adoption; it's a community hub for education, support, and connection.

We believe that everyone has something to offer in the quest for animal welfare. Whether you're looking to adopt a furry friend, volunteer your time, or support our mission through donations, your involvement is invaluable. As we move forward, we invite you to join us on the exciting journey. Visit us, engage with our community, and discover how you can contribute to a world where every animal is cherished, and every human knows the love of a pet.
