Volunteer with Us!

Join our compassionate community at Paws & Claws, where every volunteer makes a significant difference in the lives of animals and the people who love them. By volunteering with us, you become a vital part of our mission to provide care, shelter, and love to animals in need. Your time, skills, and heart can help us create a world where every animal is treated with respect and kindness.

Why Volunteer?

Make an impact: Your dedication helps us rescue, rehabilitate, and rehome countless animals. Every task, big or small, contributes to the well-being and happiness of our furry friends.

Connect with Animals: Get the opportunity to work directly with animals, providing them with care, love and socialization. Witness first-hand the difference you make in their lives.

Join a Community: Be a part of a supportive and like-minded community passionate about animal welfare. Meet new people, make friends, and unite in a shared mission.

Learn and Grow: Gain valuable experience and skills in animal care, teamwork, and non-profit operations. We provide training and support, making this a great opportunity for personal and professional development.

Volunteer Opportunities

We offer a range of volunteer roles to suit different interests and skill sets, including:

  • Animal Care and Enrichment
  • Adoption Support
  • Event Planning and Fundraising
  • Community Outreach and Education
  • Administrative Support
  • Facilities Maintenance

Whether you have a few hours a month or a day each week, your time can make a real difference!
