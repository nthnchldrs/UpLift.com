The Mission of Paws and Claws

Pets are nature's gift to humanity and it has been scientifically proven that opening homes and hearts to a pet increases our longevity and improves the overall quality of our lives and the life of the pet. At Paws and Claws our mission is to offer resources that will help you take the very best care of your current companion or of a new friend found through our streamlined pet adoption process.

Paws and Claws has over 20 years of experience to help you determine which 'adoptable' is the right fit for you and your family as well as navigating the complicated world of pet care. Stop by our new location and let the experts at Paws and Claws help you find and take care of your special furry friend!
