Uplift is a pioneering parent organization dedicated to elevating community welfare through a network of non-profits focused on animal care, social services, and envionmental stewardship. With Paws & Claws and Cheap Thrills under our umbrella, we're creating meaningful change in our communities.

Our Mission

To harness the power of collaboration and innovation to uplift lives—both human and non-human—across our communities. We envision a world where every being is valued, and every action contributes to the greater good.
