About Us - Cheap Thrills

Founded in 2011, our thrift store has become a cornerstone of the San Francisco community, offering a unique assortment of second-hand items ranging from vintage clothing and furniture to books, electronics, and much more. Our mission is simple: to provide quality goods at affordable prices while promoting sustainability and supporting our community.

At Cheap Thrills, every item on our shelves tells a story, and every purchase you make helps to write the next chapter. As a proudly local business, we're committed to giving back. All proceeds from our sales go towards supporting local initiatives and charities, making every donation and purchase a contribution to a greater cause.

Over the years, our store has grown from a modest local operation into a beloved community hub, thanks to our dedicated staff, generous donors, and loyal customers. In the spirit of our recent merger and with the support of our new parent company, we're excited to expand our online presence, making it easier than ever for you to discover the perfect finds from the comfort of your home.

Our journey since 2011 has been filled with countless thrills, and as we look to the future, we're excited to continue growing, giving, and sharing the joy of thrift with you. Whether you're looking for that one-of-a-kind item, hoping to declutter responsibly, or simply want to support a good cause, Cheap Thrills is here for you.
