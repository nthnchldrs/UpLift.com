Every item you give helps us support a good cause, making affordable treasures available to our community. Your generosity keeps our mission alive, and we're grateful for every contribution.

What We Love to Receive:

  • Good Condition Only: Items should be clean and in working order—no damages or repairs needed.
  • Quality Clothing: Gently used clothing, shoes, and accessories for all ages.
  • Household Items: Dishes, cookware, small appliances, and home decor.
  • Toys & Games: Complete sets only, clean and ready for a new home.
  • Books & Media: In readable/watchable condition, without major damage.
  • Electronics: Functioning well, with all their parts and cables.
  • Furniture: Sturdy, without significant damage.

Sorry, We Can't Take:

  • Hazardous stuff or chemicals.
  • Broken or damaged items.
  • Items that are missing parts or pieces.
  • Items that are dirty or in poor condition.
  • Big appliances/furniture: Refridgerators, stoves, mattress queen sized and above, etc...

How to Donate:

To make a donation to Cheap Thrills, we kindly ask that you start by filling out the donation form available on the website. This form will ask for some basic information about you and the item(s) you wish to donate, including a description and the condition of the item(s). Here's how it works:

  1. Fill Out the Form: Visit our website and complete the donation form below with your details and what you're donating.
  2. Await Confirmation: We'll review your submission and get back to you with a confirmation email, including instructions and a scheduled drop-off time that suits you.
  3. Drop Off Your Donations: Bring your items to Cheap Thrills at your scheduled time. If there are any large items or special considerations, we'll coordinate with you directly to ensure everything goes smoothly.

This process helps us manage donations effectively and ensures that your generous contribution is ready to find a new home as soon as possible. Thank you for taking the time to support us through your donation!
