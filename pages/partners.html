Strengthening our community through partnership

At Uplift, we believe in the power of collaboration to extend our reach and amplify our impact. Our mission to enhance community welfare and support both human and animal well-being is brought to life through our partnerships with two key organizations: Paws & Claws Humane Society and Cheap Thrills Charity Thrift Store. Each partner plays a vital role in our collective effort to create a more compassionate and supportive community.

Paws & Claws Humane Society

Paws & Claws is a cornerstone of our partnership, dedicated to the rescue, care, and adoption of animals in need. For over two decades, Paws & Claws has been a safe haven for thousands of animals, providing not just shelter but also medical care, rehabilitation, and the chance for a new life with loving families. Their commitment to animal welfare extends beyond adoption services; they offer educational programs to the community, promoting responsible pet ownership and creating a more informed and empathetic society. Paws & Claws embodies the spirit of compassion and resilience, making them an indispensable part of our mission at Uplift.

Cheap Thrills Thrift Store

Cheap Thrills Thrift Store is an innovative partner, turning second-hand shopping into a force for good. This unique thrift store serves as a funding engine for Uplift and its affiliates, with all proceeds supporting community and animal welfare programs. Beyond its financial contributions, Cheap Thrills is a community hub, offering job training and volunteer opportunities, and promoting sustainable living through the reuse and recycling of goods. The store's success is a testament to the community's support and shared values, proving that even small actions can lead to significant community benefits.s

Stronger Together.

Paws & Claws and Cheap Thrills are more than just partners; they are integral members of the Uplift family. Their unique contributions, driven by a shared vision of a better world, enable us to tackle a wide range of challenges and make a lasting difference in the lives of many. Through these partnerships, Uplift is poised to continue its vital work, fostering a community where every individual—human or animal—can thrive.
