Join the UpLift Volunteer Family

Becoming a volunteer with Uplift means joining a passionate community dedicated to making a real difference in the lives of animals and people alike. Our volunteers are the heart and soul of our mission, bringing skills, enthusiasm, and compassion to every task they undertake. Whether you're helping out at Paws & Claws Humane Society, supporting Cheap Thrills Charity Thrift Store, or engaging in community outreach programs, your contribution is invaluable.

Why Volunteer?

Make a Direct Impact: Your efforts directly benefit animals in need and support vital community services. See the tangible results of your work in the smiles and wagging tails of those you help.

Connect with Like-minded Individuals: Join a community of caring, motivated individuals who share your passion for making a difference. Build lasting friendships and professional networks.

Gain Experience and Skills: Volunteering with us provides hands-on experience in animal care, retail operations, event planning, and more. Enhance your resume and gain valuable life skills.

Find Fulfillment: Experience the joy and fulfillment that comes from giving back. Volunteering is a deeply rewarding way to contribute to a cause you care about.

Volunteer Opportunities

We offer a wide range of volunteer roles to suit various interests and skill levels, including:

  • Animal Care and Socialization: Provide direct care, exercise, and socialization for animals at Paws & Claws.
  • Event Support: Help organize and run fundraising events, community outreach, and educational programs.
  • Thrift Store Assistance: Support Cheap Thrills Charity Thrift Store operations, from sorting donations to customer service.
  • Administrative Support: Assist with clerical duties, data entry, and other behind-the-scenes tasks to keep our operations running smoothly.

How to Become a Volunteer:

Express Interest: Fill out our volunteer interest form below with your basic information and areas of interest.

Attend an Orientation: Participate in a volunteer orientation session to learn more about UpLift, our mission, and how you can contribute.

Get Involved: Once you've completed orientation, we'll match you with volunteer opportunites that align with your interests and schedule.

*submission of form does not guarantee a potential volunteer a specific work opportunity/time.
